Enhanced Security Measures

Security is at the heart of AuthFlow Authenticator. Unlike some competitors, we employ the latest cryptographic algorithms to ensure your data is protected at all times. Here are some key aspects that highlight our commitment to security:

  • SHA-256 for Passkeys: We utilize the SHA-256 hashing algorithm, renowned for its security and irreversibility. SHA-256 generates a unique, fixed-size 256-bit (32-byte) hash, making it virtually impossible to reverse-engineer the original input from the hash. This means even if a malicious actor accesses the hash, they cannot decipher your passkey.
  • Data Encryption: All data stored within AuthFlow Authenticator, including your hashed passkeys and backup information, is fully encrypted using advanced encryption standards. This ensures that your sensitive information remains confidential and secure against unauthorized access.
  • Account Backup: AuthFlow provides a seamless backup and recovery option, ensuring you never lose access to your accounts, even if your device is lost or damaged. Our secure backup system allows you to easily restore your account on a new device without compromising your security.

Database Management and Security

By prioritizing data integrity and confidentiality, we ensure that your information is safe and only accessible to you.
